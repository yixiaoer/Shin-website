ML & Coding

With a solid foundation in multiple programming languages, my coding journey has been a continuous pursuit of knowledge and skills in computer science and machine learning.

I possess a deep understanding of the principles and applications of ML, having implemented traditional machine learning algorithms from concept to code entirely from scratch.

My passion in ML drove me further into the exploration of advanced concepts within deep learning. Here, I have not only simulated automatic differentiation but also explored more about understanding and applying JAX's tracing mechanism in learn-jax-with-code.

NLP & Languages

Languages are not only mediums of communication but also mirrors reflecting diverse cultures. My enthusiasm for language learning, having embraced English, Chinese, Japanese, and currently Estonian, naturally propelled me into Natural Language Processing. My journey in NLP, especially in Large Language Models, is driven by my curiosity about languages and how they can be processed and understood by computers.

My research work includes text classification tasks to finetune models like RoBERTa, T5, and FLAN-T5 with semi-supervised learning methods, contributing to an EMNLP workshop paper. Furthermore, I involed in a survey on the trustworthiness of LLMs, contributing to safety and fairness parts.

Through my open-source project mistral-jax, I implemented the Mistral 7B model from scratch using JAX with model architecture, tensor parallelism, and various generation methods, showcasing my technical expertise and engineering prowess.

Gaming

My passion for gaming, with Divinity: Original Sin II standing out as a favorite. The passion for video games combined with my professional expertise in AI, motivated me to explore integrating AI into gaming. I have explored to introduced GPT as an agent to navigate text-based games. While my journey into combining AI with gaming is just beginning, I am eager for collaboration and further exploration in this exciting field.
